O mnie

Professional Nail Artist & Educator

Magdalena Mikurda is a renowned nail artist and educator with a passion for excellence and innovation in the beauty industry. With years of experience and a keen eye for detail, Magdalena has established herself as a leading figure in manicure techniques and nail art design.

Her unique approach combines traditional methods with cutting-edge trends, ensuring her students receive the most comprehensive and up-to-date training available.

Magdalena's work has been featured in numerous beauty publications, and her expertise is sought after by professionals and beginners alike.
